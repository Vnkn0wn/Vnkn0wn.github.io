About Me

Hello everyone, my name is Álvaro. I am a former Electronic Engineer who decided to quit his job and start studying what I have always liked... Videogames! I am very passionate about the entire process, from empathizing and defining to ideating, prototyping, and testing cool interactive and immersive experiences. Although I am currently in a designer role, it doesn't mean that I only enjoy level design. Thanks to my engineering background, programming is not an issue for me!

Projects

Cyber Racing is a board game created for the first-year Project Boarding Call at CMGT Saxion. The objective was to make a board game with entertaining mechanics. During this project, I worked on the game mechanics, promotional solutions, and game flow.

Bob´s Life is a point-and-click game created for the first-year Project First Contact at CMGT Saxion. The objective of this project was to get used to the GXP engine and C# coding. During this project, I worked on the game mechanics, level design, and promotional solutions.

Bass & Tune is an arcade videogame created for the first-year Project Lift Off at CMGT Saxion. The objective of this game was to combine the knowledge learned during previous project and implement an Arduino based controller. During this project, I worked on the game mechanics, arduino based arcade controller fishing rod, and game testing.

Call from the Past is a physics base videogame created for the first-year Project Final Approach at CMGT Saxion. The objective of this project was to implement advance physics mechanics with the GXP engine in C#. During this project, I worked on the game mechanics, level design, prototyping and game flow.
